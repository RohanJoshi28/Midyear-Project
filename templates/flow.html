Flow of the Video Feed

The code first accesses the webcam and converts each frame into grayscale. Then the frames are passed through a OpenCv face detector giving the coordinates of the face, and the frames are cropped to just the face. After that it is converted to tensors where it is passedthrough a pytorch convolutional architecture where the emotion is detected. Finally boxes labeled with the detected emotion are put onto the frame and it is sent to the video display.
